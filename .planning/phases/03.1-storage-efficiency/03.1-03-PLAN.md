<objective>
Wire the reporting and diagnostics SELECTs onto the new dictionary schema (D-01): add one
`JOIN paths` per path-reading query, convert the cross-snapshot diff to int-equality
(`prev.path_id = curr.path_id` — verified faster than the old BLOB equality via covering
index), and keep the docker-hint GLOB working under the dictionary. The Python prefix/
ancestor/mount-longest-prefix layer keeps receiving full path `bytes` and is untouched —
that invariance is the whole reason flat dictionary beat a tree shape.

Purpose: restore a fully-green suite on the new schema with PROVABLE output equivalence to
the pre-rewrite reports (no silent behavior change).
Output: rewritten `reporting/queries.py` SELECTs, `cli.py` docker-hint query, a golden
equivalence test, and repaired/extended reporting + docker tests.
</objective>
